Helpful hints

How does this site work?

This system utilizes the OpenAI API to provide responses to user queries. Given the potential for unreliable factual outputs from GPT-3, the system first identifies relevant sections of the regulations and appends them to the user's query. By instructing GPT-3 to refer to these selected regulations when generating its response, the system produces more accurate and targeted responses than GPT-3 would otherwise provide.

Which sets of regulations are used in the embeddings?

At present, the system uses the FAM, FAH, and ADS.

Are these responses completely accurate?

No. Although the use of embeddings makes these responses more accurate than what GPT-3 would otherwise provide, they can still contain factually incorrect information. You should double-check the accuracy of the responses provided.

Should I include any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) in the questions I submit?

No.
